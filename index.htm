Orillia Ontario's most beautiful Inn and Bed and Breakfast retreat along the shores of Lake Simcoe. This exquisitely renovated Victorian Inn and Spa will whet your appetite for that ultimate B&B experience with Queen size beds, fantastic ensuite baths, and fireplaces. Join us for the finest gourmet breakfasts you’ll ever have on the wrap-around porch overlooking the lake. Come and enjoy sitting in the gazebo for that perfect sunset, or just watch the world going by. Swimming, beaches, Casino Rama, biking on the Lightfoot Trail, sunset strolls along the water's edge, or just total relaxation. The Manor hosts offer local Marina & port pick up for that special night off the boat.

As you sit in the gazebo relaxing with that perfect glass of wine watching the boats go by after a full day of golf, swimming, sunbathing or shopping you'll have to wonder if there is any place better to be than at the Manor.
