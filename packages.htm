Combine your stay with us with any of the following of Orillia’s special interests. We can arrange any combination for you, and some of the activities will provide you with a discount from the normal pricing.

Boat Cruises

Enjoy a daytime/evening cruise, or an evening dinner cruise on the Island Princess along some of Ontario’s most historic and picturesque waterways. Pre-booking discounts available,
www.orilliacruises.com

Boat Rentals

Local boat rentals are a walk away at the narrows. Power boats range from 10 to 90 H.P., pre-approval is required, www.bluebeaconmarina.com
…or canoe, kayak, and paddle boat rentals for your enjoyment, www.narrowscanoe.ca

Golf Courses

Enjoy some of Lake Simcoe’s best golf courses are located in the Orillia area. Weekday and weekend specials are available,
www.hawkridgegolf.com
www.ontariogolf.info/barriegolf.htm#orillia

Restaurants

Choose from many of Orillia’s fine restaurants, www.orillia.com/index.html?op=members&div=view&pag e=1&search=restaurants

…or at Casino Rama.
www.casinorama.com/Dining.html

Opera House

Enjoy a fantastic play put on by the Orillia Opera House, or by famous booked entertainers, www.orilliaoperahouse.ca

Casino Rama Concerts

Some of the best concerts in Canada are in the Casino Rama state-of the-art entertainment center, www.casinorama.com/Entertainment.html

Orillia Events

One thing Orillia has is entertainment for the whole family, from in-water boat shows, cardboard boat races, Mariposa Folk Festival, to downtown antique cars shows,
www.orillia.com/index.html?op=localevents

Winter Sports

Winters in Orillia provide a multitude of sports and activities for the brave at heart, downhill skiing, ice fishing, cross country skiing, snowshoeing, and snowmobiling.
