Manor Suites


Creighton Manor Inn has 4 superbly decorated guest rooms all complete with ensuite bathrooms, queen size beds, fireplaces, soaker tubs, rain showers, Egyptian cotton bedding, television, DVD, and wireless internet.

Awaken to the smell of fresh brewed coffee and hot out of the oven-baked goods. A decadent gourmet breakfast will be waiting for you when you leave your superb guest room on the spacious porch watching the boats go by, on in the exquisite dining room.

Low season: November to April
High season: May thru October


Booking and Cancellation Policy

We require a Visa or MasterCard number and expiry date to confirm the reservation. In the event of a cancellation within 48 hours of the reservation date, we will make every effort to re-let the room and a $50.00 CAN cancellation fee will be charged. If the room cannot be re-let for the dates reserved, the amount of the total reservation (maximum two nights) will be applied.
